## 6. [The annotated PyTorch training loop](https://idlemachines.co.uk/essays/pytorch-training-loop)

▲ 65 分 · smaddrellmander · [14 评论](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48638120)

📝 **概要**：逐行注解 PyTorch 训练循环，详解优化器引用、梯度累积、设备移动等常见错误及规避方法。

**🔑 要点**
- **模型.to(device)** 须在构建优化器前调用，否则参数更新失效
- **scheduler.step()** 在批次循环内调用会导致每批次衰减学习率
- **torch.no_grad()** 缺失会在验证时构建自动图，导致内存 OOM
- 批量大小应为 **8 或 16 的倍数**，以对齐张量核心 tile 尺寸

💬 **讨论**：评论区认可 PyTorch 大幅降低深度学习代码冗余，但训练循环仍易因行序错误导致隐性故障；有人关注文章所在学习平台。

🏷️ `PyTorch 训练` · `调试技巧` · `深度学习`

🔥 **回复最多的评论**（GL26 · 1 回复）：

> PyTorch already does a huge job at keeping DL not verbose. It captures the complexity to allow you to make a whole GPT model with it, running on your GPU, and keep the code readable. Yes, there are a lot of breaking points, but at some point, DL is supposed to be non trivial, it handles math concepts most people don’t understand. That is why Data Scientists have a job ;)
